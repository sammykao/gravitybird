Note: Gravity in the game calculated by using Planet Radius and Mass
with Gravitational force equation: Fg = G * (Mass of Planet)/(Radius^2)

in our equation. For mathematical logistics, mass values are ratios on the slider
that's calculated in the code. (Mass of any planet in kg is many digits). Same
for radius, the km is converted to m in the code
